Elderly Care

Within our healthcare services, providing specialized care for the elderly is of utmost importance. we specifically look for nurses who have experience, knowledge, and a genuine interest in caring for elderly patients. We value nurses who are skilled in geriatric assessments, fall prevention, managing chronic conditions, promoting mobility, and providing emotional support to the elderly population.

Chronic care assistance

Farrer Park Hospital recognizes the significance of managing chronic conditions effectively, and we are seeking freelance nurses who excel in providing specialized care for patients with chronic medical needs. As a chronic care nurse with us, you will have the opportunity to make a lasting difference in the lives of patients with ongoing health challenges.

Post-hospitalisation Care

Our team of dedicated freelance nurses specializes in providing personalized care and support to patients who have recently been discharged. Whether it's assisting with medication management, wound care, or monitoring vital signs, our nurses are equipped with the skills and compassion needed to facilitate a speedy and comfortable recovery.

End-of-life Care

Our dedicated team of freelance nurses is trained to offer comprehensive support, ensuring comfort and peace for patients and their families during this sensitive time. With a focus on personalized care and empathy, we strive to create a supportive environment that honors the unique needs and wishes of each individual.
